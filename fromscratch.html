Why I Love Photography:

Photography allows me to capture beautiful moments and scenery that can be remembered forever.

I am able to pause a moment in time and look back at it forever.

Steps to Take Great Photos:

  1. Choose a subject
  2. Check lighting
  3. Use these camera settings:
    • Low ISO
    • Fast shutter speed
    • Wide aperture
  4. Take multiple shots
  5. Keep your favorite
